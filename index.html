About Me

Hello, my name is Justin. I am a hobbyist programmer and electronics developer; I have been active and consistent in these hobbies since 2021. Having been an aircraft avionics technician since 2016, and an automotive technician before that, I believe my experience has given me a solid foundation in electrical theory and electronics fundamentals.

Some past projects of mine have included:

  • an ARINC 429 signal generator for testing avionics equipment
  • a Custom ESP-32 based replacement PCB and microcontroller assembly for a digital gauge cluster and heads-up display of a 1989 Nissan 240SX
  • A laptop conversion kit for a commodore 64
My current goal is to continue to improve on my programming and electronics understanding, and become a professional in the field of embedded systems and electronics development.
